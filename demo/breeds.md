# Cat Breeds

A short, highly scientific sampling. Back to the [Field Guide](cat-field-guide.md).

## Tabby

The default build. Striped, opinionated, ships everywhere. Pairs well with a
warm laptop.

## Maine Coon

Large. Floofy. Believes it is a dog but will never admit it.

## Sphynx

Hairless and proud — the one breed `fur` cannot, ironically, render any fur for.
See the (intentionally missing) [sphynx page](sphynx.md) for a broken-link demo.

## Calico

Tricolor chaos, as catalogued in [[millions-of-cats]] — *"all black and white and
orange too."*
